What is Gris?

Gris is a game that was developed by Nomada Studio and published by Devolver Digital and is and Atmospheric Platformer.

What is Gris about?

Gris follows a lost girl in a world where she deals with painful experiences from her life. The further you progress in the story the more you learn about the experiences and unlock new abilities that allow her to have new abilities
